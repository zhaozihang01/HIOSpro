# HIOS Vision

## Mission

Build a professional AI-powered investment research terminal for global Chinese investors.

HIOS does not predict the future.

HIOS helps investors make higher-quality decisions under uncertainty through reliable data, transparent analysis, and explainable AI.

---

## Vision

Become the most trusted intelligent investment research platform for Chinese investors worldwide.

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Data First

Every conclusion must come from verifiable market data.

AI never invents numbers.

---

### 2. Explainable

Every score must have clear reasons.

Every recommendation must include supporting evidence.

---

### 3. Risk Before Return

Risk is evaluated before opportunity.

Every report must include downside analysis.

---

### 4. Long-term Architecture

Every module should be replaceable.

No page directly depends on external data providers.

---

### 5. Continuous Evolution

HIOS continuously improves through data validation and model refinement.

---

## Product Position

HIOS is not:

- a brokerage
- a trading platform
- an AI chatbot
- a stock prediction tool

HIOS is:

An AI-powered Investment Research Terminal.

---

## Target Users

- Long-term investors
- Swing traders
- Professional researchers
- Students learning investing

---

Transform market data into actionable investment research.
